{% blocktrans %}What are you?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} When you want to travel somewhere you look up that city, and pair your interests to that of a genuine local. from there you can adjust price points, services. From there it's adventure and excitement. Enjoy your trip, experience something new! {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %}Is this safe?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Meeting new people from online sometimes can be a little nerve racking. However if you use your best judgment and trust your gut, more often than not the people on the other end are wonderful. Our CEO has met up with countless locals over the last 4 years and firmly believes Tourzan.com is one of the safest services around. Some tips to help with the nerves. chat before booking, get to know your guide or tourist. Trusted users have linked social media, an address,a personal cell phone and have provided a government ID. Justifiably they deserve a trusted stamp. working trusted users is a good way to know they are solid choice. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %}Is my credit/debt card and bank account info secure?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} 100000% secure. We are working with two companies who maintain our PCI compliance. We use Braintree payments, a paypal company for processing inbound payments and Payment Rails for outbound payments. The complete card and bank info is never stored on our servers, what we do store is heavily encrypted. we have Cross site scripting, CSRF, click jacking, and sql injection preventive measures and protections in place. We also use SSL/TLS encryption to make sure your personal info cant be sniffed out of the air.
Simply put your data is incredibly safe with us. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %}I am a guide, when do I get paid?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} As long as the 5 day grace period has passed payouts are initiated on the 1st and the 15th of the month Pacific Standard Time. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %}What is the 5 day grace period?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The five day grace period is a timeframe in wich we hold the completed order from processing as a payout. This is a liability time period for tourzan. This is the timeframe which a tourist or guide can raise any concerns or complaints about the experience. {% endblocktrans %}
